What Is Living Archives?

Living Archives is a digital museum platform that transforms artifacts into living nodes of knowledge. Each entry links metadata, conservation records, scans, scholarly research, and curatorial notes, forming a dynamic, interconnected archive.

Multi-Layered Object Records

Store rich metadata, high-res imagery, 3D scans, condition reports, and linked documentation in an elegant interface.

Visual Knowledge Web

Artifacts are part of a digital constellation, showing historical, thematic, and scholarly relationships.

Inter-Museum Collaboration

Connect research and collection data across institutions for shared exhibitions and planning.
